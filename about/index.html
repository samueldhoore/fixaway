Fix Away sidewalks, roads & bike paths

About us

We are the Fix Away group. Pious, Lesley, Sam, Selin and many other Antwerp citizens who try to fix Antwerp’s streets. We love Antwerp. That’s why we created this website. We understand the issues with bike paths, roads and sidewalks. But understanding is not enough. Action must be taken. We thought of creating a platform where people who live in Antwerp can easily upload their street issues. We collect these issues and make people aware of our platform. With enough attention from the citizens, we hope to address these problems to the government. In the end, these issues should be fixed by the government.

Our vision

We want roads in Antwerp that other countries would envy. We want beautiful sidewalks and bike paths for all the citizens of Antwerp. With better sidewalks and bike paths, everyone will have a safe experience including people with wheelchairs, blind people, old people, kids and adults who regularly bike to work and school. We want regular maintenance on the roads.

Our mission

Our mission is to collect enough materials for each issue then report it professionally to the related government department. Unfortunately, our sidewalks are not good enough for wheelchair users and blind people. So sidewalks are our priority. Then the bike paths. On some streets there are no bike paths. It is very dangerous for people of all ages to bike next to cars. That's our second priority. Then, some neighborhoods don’t have sidewalks. They have to walk on the bike paths. That’s dangerous for both bikers and pedestrians. That would be our next priority.
